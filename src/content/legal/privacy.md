## 2. Local-first by design

The Tunefield application is **offline and local**. Your music files, catalogues, ratings and analysis stay on your device and are **never** sent to us or anyone else. This policy mainly concerns the website (tunefield.app / tunefield.dj), not your local use of the software.

## 3. Hosting (Vercel)

The website is hosted by **Vercel Inc.** (USA). Loading a page processes technically necessary server logs (IP address, timestamp, user agent, requested resource). Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in secure, functioning delivery). Transfers to the USA rely on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

## 4. Beta application form (Tally)

The "Apply for beta testing" form is provided by **Tally** (EU) and stores what you enter (name, email, setup details) **in the EU**. We use it only to contact you about the beta and — if you separately opt in — the launch. Legal basis: **Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent)**. You can withdraw consent anytime by emailing mark@tunefield.app.

## 9. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and to withdraw consent at any time, plus the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Contact mark@tunefield.app.

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the stated purposes or until you withdraw consent.
